Presentation Dependencies

In the following subsections, each slide is aligned to the jobs that supply its data. The slide number is specific to the Netwrix_CDSA_Presentation.pptx file, as there are slight various in page numbering with the files designed to be saved as PDFs. Many jobs contribute to multiple slides, so jobs are highlighted in bold text the first time they are listed.
